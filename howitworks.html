How it Works

A simple, transparent process to connect borrowers and lenders.

For Lenders

1. Register with KYC

Complete your registration and upload necessary KYC documents.

2. Browse Borrowers

Explore borrower profiles, risk ratings, and funding needs.

3. Negotiate & Sign

Set terms and sign loan agreements digitally.

4. Receive EMIs

Get monthly repayments directly into your bank account.

5. Recovery Support

Assistance provided in case of payment defaults.

For Borrowers

1. Register & Pay

Create an account with zero registration fee.

2. Submit Documents

Upload income proof, bank statements, and identity documents.

3. Negotiate

Get offers from lenders and finalize mutually agreed terms.

4. Sign Agreement

Sign the agreement digitally and receive the loan amount.

5. Repay EMIs

Make timely EMI payments through auto-debit or bank transfer.
